Flint and Steel

Description

Flint and steel is a tool used to light fires. Flint and steel is very versatile as it can be used for many things.

It can be used to prime TNT in order to cause it to explode. Also, it lights the Nether Portal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Flint
  • 1 Iron Ingot

Steps

  1. Open the default crafting menu
  2. Select the iron ingot and put it in one of 4 available slots
  3. Select the flitn and put it in one of the 3 available slots
  4. Retrieve Flint and Steel on the right of the crafting menu
