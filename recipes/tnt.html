TNT

Description

TNT is an explosive block that can be primed to generate an explosion. The TNT is mostly red with white in the middle. The words "TNT" is printed on the block.

The most common use of a TNT block is to cause destruction of buildings or land. Other uses of TNT could be used to mine more efficiently, however it requires a lot of resources to create.

Ingredients

  • 1 Crafting Table
  • 5 Gunpowder
  • 4 Sand

Steps

  1. Place the crafting table and open the crafting menu
  2. Select the gunpowder in your inventory and put it in every corner of the crafting table
  3. Put the last gunpowder in the middle
  4. Fill in the rest of the missing spaces with the sand
  5. Retrieve the TNT on the right of the crafting menu
